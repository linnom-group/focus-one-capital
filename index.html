About Company

Who are we

FOCUS ONE CAPITAL was established post the Covid-19 pandemic that wreaked havoc to the personal finance of most South African citizens. We took it upon ourselves to be one with the people and stand with them in the storm. Thus, establishing an entity that assists our people in being active participants of the economy and not finding them left behind or restricted in making a meaningful life. We are not your ordinary debt specialists; we consider ourselves as experts that are one with their customers to restore and repair their credit health for the greater good.
